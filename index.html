About Me

Hello, My name is Jacob Pearsall. I have long been the avid computer user. I began to dive more into the "how" of computers when I was in 9th grade. In the schools AFJROTC department I was in charge of the website. The next summer I attended a weeklong class on HTML. I continued to develop the website over the next year, and then left the AFJROTC program. I didn't touch any coding again until college.

In college I studied Computer Science for a little bit, I changed majors a few times and realized I didn't know what I wanted to do. In 2013 I took a "break" from college to do an internship at Walt Disney World. This internship turned into a job and I left college behind for many years. In 2020 when the world shut down I decided to continue my education. I finished my Bachelors in Computer Science in May 2021. Not having any idea as to what came next, and working for Disney who pays for my education, I decided to pursue my Masters.
